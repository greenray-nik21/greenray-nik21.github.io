Research
A Hybrid Pain Assessment Approach with Stacked Autoencoders and Attention-Based CP-LSTM
Sagnik De, Prithwijit Mukherjee, Anisha Halder Roy

International Conference on Ambient Intelligence, Knowledge Informatics and Industrial Electronics (AIKIIE), 2023

This study centers on the examination of pain perception in individuals through the analysis of EEG signals. A novel hybrid deep learning algorithm, denoted as Stacked Autoencoder-ATTN-CPLSTM, has been devised for the prediction of the two distinct phases of pain perception in an individual by leveraging EEG signals. Additionally, this research investigates alterations in brain wave patterns, including delta wave (0.5-4 Hz), theta wave (4-8 Hz), alpha wave (8-12 Hz), and beta wave (12-35 Hz), across various brain regions during these three phases of pain perception.

A Novel Human Stress Level Detection Technique Using EEG
Sagnik De, Dipanjan Konar, Prithwijit Mukherjee, Anisha Halder Roy

IEEE International Conference on Network, Multimedia and Information Technology (NMITCON), 2023

In this study, first EEG signals of the subjects have been recorded while they solve four mathematical question sets with different complexity levels. Subsequently, different handcrafted feature extraction techniques have been employed for extracting eight features, namely Skewness, Kurtosis, Maximum, Mean, Mean Absolute Value, Minimum, Standard Deviation, and Power Spectral Density from the pre-processed EEG signals. A majority voting-based ensemble classifier model has been designed by combining the predictions of three different classifiers, to predict a person‚Äôs mental stress level. The obtained classification accuracy of the proposed ensemble classifier model is 93.85 %.
