Research
Pain Perception
Sagnik De, Prithwijit Mukherjee, Anisha Halder Roy
Technical Report, 2009

This study centers on the examination of pain perception in individuals through the analysis of EEG signals. A novel hybrid deep learning algorithm, denoted as SE-ATTN-modified LSTM, has been devised for the prediction of the three distinct phases of pain perception in an individual by leveraging EEG signals. Additionally, this research investigates alterations in brain wave patterns, including delta wave (0.5-4 Hz), theta wave (4-8 Hz), alpha wave (8-12 Hz), and beta wave (12-35 Hz), across various brain regions during these three phases of pain perception.
